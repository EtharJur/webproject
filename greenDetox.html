Green Detox Juice

Description: A healthy detox juice packed with cucumber, spinach, green apple, and lemon, perfect for boosting energy and hydration.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cucumber (sliced)
  • 1 green apple (cored and sliced)
  • 1 cup spinach
  • 1/2 lemon (juiced)
  • 1 inch ginger root (peeled)
  • 1/2 cup water
  • Ice cubes (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Blend the cucumber, green apple, spinach, lemon juice, and ginger in a blender.
  2. Add water to help blend the ingredients smoothly.
  3. Serve chilled with ice cubes if desired.
