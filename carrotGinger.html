Carrot Ginger Juice

Description: A zesty and invigorating juice made with carrots, ginger, and lemon for a refreshing boost of vitamins and flavor.

Ingredients:

  • 4 large carrots (peeled and chopped)
  • 1 inch ginger root (peeled)
  • 1/2 lemon (juiced)
  • 1 tsp honey (optional)
  • 1/2 cup water
  • Ice cubes (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Juice the carrots and ginger using a juicer.
  2. Pour the juice into a glass and stir in lemon juice.
  3. If desired, add honey to sweeten the juice.
  4. Serve chilled with ice cubes if desired.
