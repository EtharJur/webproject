Caesar Salad

Description: A classic Caesar salad with crispy romaine lettuce, parmesan cheese, crunchy croutons, and homemade Caesar dressing.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups romaine lettuce (washed and chopped)
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese (grated)
  • 1/2 cup croutons
  • 1/4 cup Caesar dressing
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp garlic (minced)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Wash and chop the romaine lettuce.
  2. In a bowl, combine the lettuce, parmesan cheese, and croutons.
  3. In a small pan, heat olive oil and saut√© minced garlic until fragrant.
  4. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss until everything is evenly coated.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste and serve immediately.
