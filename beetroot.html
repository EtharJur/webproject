Beetroot Juice

Description: A nutritious and detoxifying juice made with fresh beets, carrots, and a hint of ginger. Full of vitamins and minerals!

Ingredients:

  • 2 medium beets (peeled and chopped)
  • 2 carrots (peeled and chopped)
  • 1 inch ginger root (peeled)
  • 1/2 lemon (juiced)
  • 1 tbsp honey (optional)
  • 1/2 cup water
  • Ice cubes (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Juice the beets, carrots, and ginger using a juicer.
  2. Pour the juice into a glass and add lemon juice.
  3. If you like it sweeter, stir in honey to taste.
  4. Add ice cubes if desired, and serve chilled.
