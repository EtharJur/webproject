Greek Salad

Description: A refreshing Greek salad with cucumber, tomatoes, olives, and feta cheese. Itâ€™s perfect for a light meal or a side dish.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cucumber (sliced)
  • 2 cups tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1/2 cup Kalamata olives
  • 1/2 cup feta cheese (crumbled)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Combine cucumber, tomatoes, olives, and feta cheese in a large bowl.
  2. Drizzle with olive oil and red wine vinegar.
  3. Sprinkle with oregano, salt, and pepper.
  4. Toss everything together gently and serve chilled.
